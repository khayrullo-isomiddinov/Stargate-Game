THE MISSION

Deep in the heart of an ancient, nameless desert, explorers have uncovered something extraordinary — a Stargate: a device of unknown origin, rumoured to open portals across space and time.

A violent sandstorm tore it apart. Three components were scattered across the dunes and buried under the sand:

The Watch Controls temporal calibration — without it, the gate opens to nowhere.
The Robot The mechanical heart of the Stargate. It drives the rotation sequence.
The Camera Locks the dimensional coordinates. Without it, there is no destination.

Previous expeditions left directional clues in the sand. Rare oases offer water — but the desert is merciless, and not every oasis is what it seems.

You have limited water. Every turn costs a drop.
Find all three components before the desert claims you.

How to Play

Survive the desert. Recover the Stargate components.

👣

Move

Click any glowing tile adjacent to you to move there. Costs 1 action.

⛏️

Dig

Stand on a tile and press Dig to reveal what's underneath. Costs 1 action.

💧

Water

You lose 1 water every time you end your turn. Hit 0 and it's game over.

🌴

Oases

Dig an oasis to fully refill your water. Watch out — one of the four is a drought!

🗺️

Clues

Each part has 2 directional clues hidden nearby. Find both before you can dig up the part.

🏆

Victory

Recover all 3 Stargate parts — Watch, Robot, Camera — before your water runs dry.
